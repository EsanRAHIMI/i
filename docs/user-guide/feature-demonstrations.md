## Voice Interaction Demonstrations

### Basic Voice Commands

#### Demonstration 1: Simple Scheduling
**Scenario**: You need to schedule a team meeting

**Voice Command**: "Schedule a team meeting for next Tuesday at 10 AM"

**AI Response**: "I'll schedule a team meeting for Tuesday, January 16th at 10:00 AM. Should I invite anyone specific to this meeting?"

**Follow-up**: "Yes, invite Sarah, Mike, and Jennifer"

**AI Response**: "Perfect! I've scheduled a team meeting for Tuesday, January 16th at 10:00 AM and sent invitations to Sarah, Mike, and Jennifer. The meeting is set for Conference Room A. Is there anything else you'd like me to add?"

**Key Learning Points**:
- Be specific with dates and times
- The AI will ask clarifying questions
- You can add details in follow-up commands

#### Demonstration 2: Complex Scheduling with Constraints
**Scenario**: You need to schedule a client presentation with multiple constraints

**Voice Command**: "I need to schedule a client presentation with ABC Corp next week, but I need 2 hours, and it can't be on Wednesday"

**AI Response**: "I'll help you schedule a 2-hour client presentation with ABC Corp next week, avoiding Wednesday. Let me check your availability... I found these options:
- Tuesday, January 15th: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- Thursday, January 17th: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM  
- Friday, January 18th: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
Which works best for you?"

**Your Response**: "Thursday morning looks good"

**AI Response**: "Excellent! I've scheduled your client presentation with ABC Corp for Thursday, January 17th from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. I've also blocked 30 minutes before for preparation. Would you like me to send a calendar invitation to ABC Corp?"

**Key Learning Points**:
- The AI understands complex constraints
- It provides multiple options
- It can automatically add preparation time

## Troubleshooting Demonstrations

### Demonstration 10: Handling Voice Recognition Issues

#### Problem: AI Doesn't Understand Names
**Voice Command**: "Schedule a meeting with Xiaowei Zhang"

**AI Response**: "I didn't catch that name clearly. Could you spell it for me or try saying it more slowly?"

**Solution 1 - Spelling**: "Schedule a meeting with X-I-A-O-W-E-I Z-H-A-N-G"

**Solution 2 - Phonetic**: "Schedule a meeting with Shao-way Zhang"

**Solution 3 - Add to Dictionary**: 
1. Go to Settings â†’ Voice â†’ Custom Names
2. Add "Xiaowei Zhang" with phonetic spelling
3. Practice pronunciation with AI

#### Problem: Misunderstood Time
**Voice Command**: "Schedule lunch at 8" (meaning 8 PM)

**AI Response**: "I've scheduled lunch for 8:00 AM tomorrow. That seems early for lunch - did you mean 8:00 PM?"

**Correction**: "Yes, 8 PM"

**AI Response**: "Got it! I've updated the lunch to 8:00 PM tomorrow."

### Demonstration 11: Calendar Sync Issues

#### Problem: Events Not Syncing
**Symptoms**: 
- AI doesn't see recent calendar events
- Created events don't appear in Google Calendar

**Troubleshooting Steps**:

1. **Check Connection Status**:
   - Go to Settings â†’ Integrations â†’ Google Calendar
   - Look for green "Connected" status

2. **Manual Sync**:
   - Click "Sync Now" button
   - Wait for confirmation message

3. **Refresh Connection**:
   - Click "Disconnect" then "Reconnect"
   - Re-authorize permissions

4. **Test with Voice Command**:
   - "Refresh my calendar"
   - "What meetings do I have today?"

**AI Response**: "I've refreshed your calendar connection. I can now see your recent events including the client call you added this morning."
