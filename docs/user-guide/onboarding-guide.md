# User Onboarding Guide - Welcome to Your AI Assistant

Welcome to your personal AI Assistant! This guide will help you get started and make the most of your intelligent digital companion.

## What is Your AI Assistant?

Your AI Assistant is a next-generation agentic AI system designed to act as your conscious digital twin. It learns from your interactions, understands your preferences, and helps you optimize your daily life through:

- **Voice Interactions**: Natural conversations using speech-to-text and text-to-speech
- **Calendar Management**: Intelligent scheduling and event coordination
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Confirmations and updates via your preferred messaging app
- **Privacy-First Learning**: Personalized AI that keeps your data secure and private

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Visit the Application**: Go to your AI Assistant web portal
2. **Sign Up**: Click "Create Account" and provide:
   - Email address
   - Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Time zone
   - Language preference (English, Persian, Arabic)
3. **Verify Email**: Check your email and click the verification link
4. **Complete Profile**: Add your name and preferences

### Step 2: Initial Setup

#### Voice Configuration
Your AI Assistant learns your voice patterns for better recognition:

1. **Microphone Permission**: Allow microphone access when prompted
2. **Voice Calibration**: Complete the voice setup wizard:
   - Say the provided phrases clearly
   - Speak in your normal tone and pace
   - Test in a quiet environment first

**Voice Interaction Examples:**
- "Schedule a meeting with John tomorrow at 3 PM"
- "What's on my calendar today?"
- "Remind me to call mom at 6 PM"
- "Cancel my 2 PM appointment"

#### Privacy Settings
Configure your privacy preferences:

1. **Data Processing Consent**: Choose what data the AI can learn from
2. **Voice Training**: Opt-in to federated learning for better personalization
3. **Communication Preferences**: Set how you want to receive notifications

### Step 3: Connect Your Services

#### Google Calendar Integration

1. **Navigate to Integrations**: Go to Settings ‚Üí Integrations
2. **Connect Google Calendar**: Click "Connect Google Calendar"
3. **Authorize Access**: Sign in to Google and grant calendar permissions
4. **Sync Settings**: Choose which calendars to sync
5. **Test Integration**: Ask your AI to "show me today's schedule"

**What the AI can do with your calendar:**
- Read existing events and appointments
- Create new events based on voice commands
- Suggest optimal meeting times
- Send meeting reminders
- Handle scheduling conflicts intelligently

#### WhatsApp Business Integration (Optional)

1. **Opt-in to WhatsApp**: Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications
2. **Provide Phone Number**: Enter your WhatsApp number
3. **Verify Connection**: Respond to the verification message
4. **Set Preferences**: Choose what notifications to receive

**WhatsApp Features:**
- Meeting confirmations and reminders
- Daily schedule summaries
- Action confirmations (Y/N responses)
- Important alerts and updates

## Voice Interaction Guide

### Best Practices for Voice Commands

#### Speaking Clearly
- **Pace**: Speak at a normal, conversational pace
- **Clarity**: Pronounce words clearly, especially names and times
- **Environment**: Use in quiet spaces when possible
- **Distance**: Stay within 3 feet of your microphone

#### Effective Command Structure

**Good Examples:**
- "Schedule a team meeting for next Tuesday at 10 AM in Conference Room A"
- "Move my 3 PM appointment to 4 PM tomorrow"
- "What meetings do I have this week with Sarah?"
- "Block 2 hours tomorrow morning for focused work"

**Less Effective:**
- "Um, can you maybe schedule something?" (too vague)
- "Meeting John sometime next week" (missing details)
- Speaking too fast or mumbling

## Privacy and Consent Guide

### Understanding Your Data

#### What Data is Collected
- **Voice Interactions**: Audio recordings and transcriptions
- **Calendar Data**: Events, attendees, locations, times
- **Usage Patterns**: How and when you use the system
- **Preferences**: Your settings and customizations

#### How Your Data is Protected
- **Local Processing**: Voice recognition happens on your device when possible
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Federated Learning**: AI improves without sharing your raw data
- **Access Controls**: Only you can access your personal data

### Your Privacy Rights

#### Data Access
You can request a complete copy of your data at any time:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Data Export
2. Click "Request Data Export"
3. Receive download link via email within 48 hours

#### Data Correction
Update or correct your information:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Profile
2. Edit any incorrect information
3. Changes are applied immediately

#### Data Deletion
Delete your account and all associated data:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Account Deletion
2. Confirm deletion request
3. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days

## Advanced Features

### Intelligent Scheduling

#### Smart Conflict Resolution
When scheduling conflicts arise, your AI Assistant:
- Identifies conflicting appointments
- Suggests alternative times
- Considers travel time between locations
- Respects your priority settings

**Example Interaction:**
```
You: "Schedule a client meeting for Tuesday at 2 PM"
AI: "I notice you have a team meeting at 2 PM on Tuesday. Would you like me to:
    1. Move the team meeting to 3 PM
    2. Schedule the client meeting for 1 PM instead
    3. Find another day that works for both"
You: "Move the team meeting to 3 PM"
AI: "Done! I've moved your team meeting to 3 PM and scheduled the client meeting for 2 PM on Tuesday."
```

#### Availability Intelligence
Your AI learns your preferences:
- **Preferred meeting times**: Learns when you're most productive
- **Buffer time**: Automatically adds travel time between meetings
- **Focus blocks**: Protects time for deep work
- **Personal time**: Respects your work-life boundaries

### Federated Learning Benefits

#### How It Works
- Your AI model trains locally on your device
- Only encrypted model updates are shared (never your raw data)
- The global model improves while preserving your privacy
- You benefit from collective intelligence without compromising privacy

#### Privacy Guarantees
- **Differential Privacy**: Mathematical guarantee of privacy protection
- **Secure Aggregation**: Your individual data cannot be reconstructed
- **Local Control**: You control what data is used for learning
- **Transparency**: Full visibility into what data is processed

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Voice Recognition Problems

**Issue**: AI doesn't understand my voice commands
**Solutions:**
- Speak more slowly and clearly
- Reduce background noise
- Check microphone permissions
- Recalibrate your voice profile in Settings

**Issue**: AI misunderstands names or specific terms
**Solutions:**
- Add custom pronunciations in Settings ‚Üí Voice ‚Üí Custom Terms
- Spell out difficult names: "Schedule meeting with J-O-H-N"
- Use phonetic spelling: "Schedule meeting with John, spelled J as in Juliet, O-H-N"

### Calendar Integration Issues

**Issue**: Calendar events not syncing
**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh calendar connection in Settings ‚Üí Integrations
- Verify Google Calendar permissions
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting your calendar

**Issue**: AI creates duplicate events
**Solutions:**
- Check for multiple calendar connections
- Review sync settings
- Clear cache and refresh

### WhatsApp Integration Issues

**Issue**: Not receiving WhatsApp notifications
**Solutions:**
- Verify phone number is correct
- Check WhatsApp is installed and active
- Confirm opt-in status in Settings ‚Üí Notifications
- Check message delivery status
